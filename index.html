Chuck E. Cheeses

I made my television debut in this national spot for Chuck E. Cheeses new, more adult friendly menu. I didn't get to eat any of the pizza, but I did get to spend the day in a Chuck E. Cheeses - so I'm not complaining.

Bacon Soup

I lent my voice to Clancy, the woodsy soup purveyor in this short animation I made with my girlfriend, Caroline. It was this silly little weekend project that got me interested pursuing a career in voiceover and seeing what other kinds of characters I can lend my voice to.
